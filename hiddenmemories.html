Hidden Memories

A 3D first person horror/upgrade style game made in Unity.

Hidden Memories is a first person horror game made in Unity. The objective is to escape a maze by finding keys to unlock doors, while fighting enemies along the way using guns you find through the game. There is a pistol, shotgun and assault rifle which I really enjoyed programming. The player has a soul meter that drains over time, forcing the player to keep fighting to survive. If you die from attacks or your soul meter deplenishing it ends a run, where player's can choose between multiple upgrades. This project was a heavy scope for me, and I didn't have time to implement every feature I had initially planned. However, it was rewarding and I learned a lot, especially around gameplay programming concerning first person movement and weapons. If I could do it all over again, I would have taken the time to carefully plan out architecture so that it's more extensible.
