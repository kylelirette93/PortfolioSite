Fruit Dash

A 3D endless runner made in Unity.

Fruit Dash was the result of 2 days of passion. I participated in Mini Jam 198 with two artists, a novice programmer and sound designer. I took on the role of both project lead and lead programmer, managing tasks through trello. I was happy with the outcome, we had a fully playable game, which is always a success and we had enough time to add some polish. I gained valuable experience working with splines and animation curves in order to handle movement. I had one spline in the center lane and offset the position when the player moved left or right. I'm hoping to release it on the play store eventually! Check out the game on itch!
