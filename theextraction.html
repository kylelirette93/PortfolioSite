The Extraction

The Extraction is a year long project where I get to work on a team of 5. I've taken on both Sound Designer and Programmer for the project. The soundtrack is entirely original music and I got to learn a new software called Reaper through this project. The voice acting for enemies and Tony is done by yours truly and some of the sound effects in the game are foley. I programmed a level manager, interaction system, enemy AI, audio system, dialogue system, powerups and a custom editor to assist other developer's among many other tasks.
