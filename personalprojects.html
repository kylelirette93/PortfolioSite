Fruit Dash


Fruit Dash was a game jam submission for Mini Jam 198 on Itch IO. I worked on a team with two artists and a sound designer. Additionally I had the opportunity to mentor someone who hadn't programmed before. It's an endless runner where you collect fruits to recharge your stamina meter. When you dash you gain speed, which is your score.
