Hey, I'm Kyle! I'm a game developer from Nova Scotia, Canada and just starting my second year of Game Programming at NSCC. I've been entrenched in video games since I was five years old. My first experience was with retro consoles such as Super Nintendo and N64. When I was young, I explored web development to create Simpson's fan pages, which I loved doing. As The Simpson's fan community slowly faded into the backdrop, so did a part of me. However, through the years I continued pondering how games were brought to life. I pursued various career paths over 30 years of my life in search of something I couldn't grasp leaving me with a void. One day I was called to make a change and downloaded Unity. From that point I realized my true passion was staring me in the face my whole life and I've never looked back. Curious about the games I've worked on?

C#, Github, Visual Studio, Unity, Unreal Engine, Visual Scripting, Particle Systems.
